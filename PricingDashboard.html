Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions about our plans.

Can I switch plans later? expand_more
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for Human Pro? expand_more
We offer a discounted first session for Human Pro, but due to the personalized nature of 1-on-1 coaching, we do not offer a free trial. The AI Basic plan does have a 7-day free trial.
How does the Hybrid review work? expand_more
In the Hybrid plan, our AI analyzes your daily workouts. Once a week, a certified human trainer reviews your weekly progress data and sends you a personalized video message.
